 How do you put together my needs with a workable plan?

I find it extremely important to find out many details about you (and / or your company) before we get started. Just like I think it's important that a doctor know more than your current and present symptoms, I think it's important to know you, where you've come from and where you want to head, before working through your development needs. Everything is built on top of something before that and knowing that history is relevant to putting together our best solution.
